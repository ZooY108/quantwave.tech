Executive Summary

QuantWaveTech is a Singapore-based quantitative investment management firm focused on the development and deployment of systematic trading strategies across U.S. financial markets. All trading operations are executed via Interactive Brokers Prime through a dedicated server infrastructure. Algorithm research and systems engineering are supported by a Zurich-based development team.

Investment Approach

QuantWaveTech combines quantitative research, systematic execution, and strict risk management to identify and capture opportunities across liquid U.S. markets.

  • Automated signal generation and portfolio construction
  • Multi-timeframe analysis — 1h / 30m / 15m / 5m / 1m

  • Real-time position management and risk monitoring
  • Performance analytics and drawdown controls
  • Robust, repeatable processes designed for changing market conditions
